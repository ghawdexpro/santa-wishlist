# OPERATION: NICE LIST
## The Santa Experience - Tarantino Edition (Kid-Friendly)

> A cinematic, non-linear Christmas heist story where YOUR CHILD is the target of the most important mission of the year.

---

## OVERVIEW

**Total Runtime:** ~90-120 seconds (video) + Live Call
**Tone:** Spy thriller meets Christmas magic - think Spy Kids, not Kill Bill
**Style:** Quick cuts, dramatic reveals, title cards, multiple POVs

## CHAPTER 4: THE MESSAGE

**Duration:** 30 seconds
**Type:** PERSONALIZED (HeyGen Talking Avatar)
**POV:** Santa direct-to-camera

### Story Beat
The emotional payoff. Santa speaks DIRECTLY to the child. Personal, warm, breaking the "spy movie" fourth wall to deliver genuine Christmas magic.

### Visual
```
Santa in his cozy office setting (HeyGen avatar background).
Direct eye contact with camera.
Warm, intimate framing - like a video call from Santa.
Fireplace visible in background, soft lighting.
```

### HeyGen Script Template
```
SANTA (warm, direct, personal):

"Ho ho ho... [CHILD'S NAME]."

[Warm smile]

"Yes, I'm talking to YOU."

"My elves tell me you've been absolutely wonderful this year.
Especially with [GOOD_BEHAVIOR]. That made it straight to
the top of my Nice List."

[Slightly serious but kind]

"Now, I also heard about [THING_TO_IMPROVE]. And you know what?
That's okay. Even my elves are still learning. What matters is
that you're trying."

[Big smile]

"And I know you want to [THING_TO_LEARN]. Well, let me tell you
a secret..."

[Leans in conspiratorially]

"I believe in you. And so does everyone at the North Pole."

[CUSTOM_MESSAGE if provided]

[Sits back, warm]

"I'll see you VERY soon, [CHILD'S NAME].
The elves are always watching."

[Winks]

"Merry Christmas."
```

### Audio Notes
- Use HeyGen Santa avatar with warm, grandfatherly voice
- Pacing: conversational, not rushed
- Emotional beats: impressed → caring → encouraging → warm

## CHAPTER 6: THE DEBRIEF (LIVE CALL)

**Duration:** 3-5 minutes
**Type:** LIVE INTERACTIVE (HeyGen Streaming Avatar)
**POV:** Real-time conversation

### Story Setup
After the video, the child can have a LIVE video call with Santa. This is framed as a "debrief" - Santa checking in with his newest "agent."

### Santa's Opening (Streaming Avatar Greeting)
```
SANTA:
"Agent [CHILD'S NAME], this is Santa Claus.
Secure channel established."

[Warm laugh]

"Just kidding - but it IS really me!
I wanted to check in personally. Did you get my message?"

[Waits for response]

"So tell me... what would you like for Christmas?"
```

### Conversation Context (passed to Gemini)
```json
{
  "child_name": "[NAME]",
  "child_age": [AGE],
  "good_behavior": "[BEHAVIOR]",
  "thing_to_improve": "[IMPROVE]",
  "thing_to_learn": "[LEARN]",
  "custom_message": "[MESSAGE]",
  "conversation_style": "warm, grandfatherly, encouraging, playful spy references",
  "topics_to_reference": [
    "The scout mission to their home",
    "Their achievements from the dossier",
    "The workshop preparing their gift",
    "Christmas magic and believing"
  ]
}
```

### Sample Conversation Flows

**If child mentions a toy:**
```
"Ah yes, I may have seen that going through the Workshop today...
My elves are VERY good at keeping secrets though.
You'll have to wait until Christmas morning!"
```

**If child asks about elves:**
```
"The elves? Oh, they're wonderful! Scout Team Alpha specifically
asked me to say hello to you. They were very impressed with
your Christmas tree, by the way."
```

**If child asks about reindeer:**
```
"Rudolph is doing great! He's been training extra hard this year.
Between you and me, he's excited to fly over your house on
Christmas Eve."
```

### Sign-off
```
SANTA:
"Well, Agent [CHILD'S NAME], it's been wonderful talking to you.
Remember - keep being good, keep believing, and I'll see you
VERY soon."

[Salutes playfully]

"This is Santa Claus, signing off. Over and out!"

[Winks]

"Merry Christmas!"
```

## APPENDIX: QUICK REFERENCE

### Personalization Variables
- `[CHILD_NAME]` - Child's first name
- `[CHILD_AGE]` - Age in years
- `[GOOD_BEHAVIOR]` - What they did well
- `[THING_TO_IMPROVE]` - Area for growth (gentle)
- `[THING_TO_LEARN]` - Goal they're working toward
- `[CUSTOM_MESSAGE]` - Parent's optional message
- `[CITY_NAME]` - Location for Scout chapter
- `[USER_ROOM_PHOTO]` - Parent's uploaded room image

### Tone Guidelines
- **Spy thriller** framing but always WARM and FUN
- Never scary, never threatening
- "Elves watching" = caring, not creepy
- Santa is wise, warm, encouraging
- The "mission" is delivering JOY

### Music Cues
- Chapter 0: Dramatic orchestral hit
- Chapter 1: Sneaky/spy music (think Pink Panther meets Christmas)
- Chapter 2: Thoughtful, building tension
- Chapter 3: Upbeat, industrious, magical
- Chapter 4: Warm, emotional, intimate
- Chapter 5: Triumphant, joyful resolution
